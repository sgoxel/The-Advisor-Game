The Advisor Game

HTML5 + WebGL Based Game Demo

🎮 Game Link (Under Development: [Play Now](https://sgoxel.github.io/The-Advisor-Game/)

🌍 Theme

Medieval + Fantasy Roleplaying & Strategy Game
- Begin as a humble peasant and rise through the ranks: Knight → Lord → King → Emperor.
- Unlike traditional RPGs, you won’t directly control armies or trade companies. Instead, your decisions shape the world, while AI-driven characters act independently.

🧠 The King’s Advisor (Core Focus)

- The Advisor is the centerpiece of the game — a character roleplayed by an LLM (Large Language Model).
- The Advisor provides guidance to the King on economy, people, army, and international relations.
- Dialogues are dynamically generated by AI, making every interaction unique.
- Players can directly converse with the Advisor, issuing commands or asking for recommendations.
- Your choices, combined with the Advisor’s counsel, determine the fate of your kingdom.

⚔️ Gameplay Mechanics

- Diplomacy: Negotiate alliances, treaties, or declare wars.
- Economy: Manage taxes, trade routes, and financial stability.
- Military: Armies act based on Advisor-driven strategies rather than micromanagement.
- City Building: Expand from villages to castles and eventually imperial capitals.

🎨 Technical Features

- 2.5D AI-generated characters for immersive storytelling.
- Lightweight design ensures smooth play online or offline.
- Long-term focus on mechanics and AI-driven interactions, with visuals evolving later.

🚀 Vision

The Advisor Game reimagines RPG and strategy genres by placing the player in the role of a decision-making ruler guided by an AI Advisor.
- Your Advisor becomes your strategist, diplomat, and voice of reason.
- Every policy, war, or alliance is shaped through interactive AI-driven dialogue.
- The ultimate goal: a lightweight, accessible, yet deeply strategic experience where leadership is about wisdom, not micromanagement.
